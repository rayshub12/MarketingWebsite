MnM is one of the top success stories in India today. We serve more customers than any other company in India - 7500 clients with 80% of Fortune Global 2000. Our product “Knowledge Store” is focused on influencing revenue decisions of all b2b companies in the world. We take an extended view to identify new revenue sources of clients, their client’s and their client’s clients. More than 97000 companies have approached us till date with their pain points around revenue growth. Consequently, we have an extremely sticky non-linear revenue model with over 95% renewal rates. We have been growing 100% Year on Year and our future growth projections are exponentially higher by leveraging our product.

Challenge your own experience by identifying new revenue sources for all fortune companies and market leaders across major industries. All you require is a ‘go getting’ attitude and strong business acumen to see dollars and show dollars.

We invite you to build the World’s largest Revenue Impact company.

Client Service Head (Chicago, USA)

Apply here !

Location: Chicago, USA
Experience required: 6 to 12 year's of full-time experience in a similar role.

If you have a strong business acumen and articulation to challenge the revenue growth strategies of the top 95 of 100 global companies in each industry (provided you have access to our proprietary insights on all high growth technologies and use cases impacting their business), then join us.

Who are we?

MarketsandMarkets™ is the world’s largest revenue impact company, serving over 5,000 customers that include 80% of the top 2,000 companies in the industries we track. In the face of constant technological innovations and market disruption, we help organizations plan and operationalize their future revenue mix decisions by identifying over 30,000 high growth opportunities ranging from USD 1 billion to USD 500 billion. Our revenue impact methodology provides actionable and quantified insights on converged, granular, and networked market ecosystems that result from disruptive technologies and high growth markets. We provide an extended lens on not only what impacts our client’s revenue, but also what impacts our clients’ clients’ revenue, thus uncovering latent opportunities. Organizations choose us to stay ahead of the curve and accelerate their hot-bet revenue decisions and implementations in 6–12 months, giving them a unique first-mover innovation advantage. Knowledge Store (our IP), has a 95% renewal rate, which is helping us grow 100% year-on-year and influence the revenue decisions of all major B2B companies in the world. From here, simple mathematics makes us a billion-dollar enterprise, leveraging our existing research IP by almost 100%. 50 Associate Client Partners will write nearly half this story.

No company in India has as many B2B clients as we have.

India’s story was written on re-engineering cost. We will rewrite this by revenue augmentation for global B2B companies in the world.

"As you are unique for us, we are unique for you as well. "

What will your job look like?

This is an exciting role which will continue to evolve:

  • The role is fast-paced and the candidate should be willing to get into hands-on work as per the need (mine the account for leads; cold calling/email).
  • Collaborate and engage with the team in India for business and client deliverables.
  • Generate new opportunities within existing accounts
  • Identify other strategic areas for revenue within existing client organizations to develop more client relationships.
  • Manage research projects for clients such as market sizing, pricing analytics, competitive landscape assessment, and benchmarking studies by coordinating between the client, onsite, and research teams
  • Coordinate and lead the entire business development process—introductions, presentations, proposals, and closing.

All you need is...

  • 6–12 years’ of full-time experience in a similar role
  • A master’s degree
  • Person should ideally be based in Chicago (or willing to relocate there)
  • Candidates with prior experience in services, product, and research are encouraged to apply
  • The ideal profile would have experience in account management (or sales but with examples where they have nurtured client relationships over a long period)
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced work environment
  • Exceptional professional presence and high business acumen

Our Culture

Culture at MnM is defined with the word GIVE- whatever you GIVE will come back to you ten times; whether it is about giving love, knowledge, authority , it comes back to you ten times. GIVE stands for G (Growth), I (Innovation), V (Velocity), E (Excellence); you give any of these, it will come back to ten times.

We believe we will create world’s most inspiring company by GIVING growth to B2B companies which will bring growth to MnM.

With revenue mix of companies changing, product life cycle getting shorter, disruptions impacting every businesses, greenfield use cases blurring the boundaries of legacy and startups, industries getting converged, biggest opportunity for MnM is to provide insights to all b2b companies ahead of time in terms of what is likely to impact their business and how their innovations can impact others. We are exclusively poised to provide Known ‘Unknown’ insights and ‘Unknown Unknown’ insights in terms of detailed market potential of known markets and unknown markets .

Unknown markets are the latent demand which companies haven’t explored yet. Majority of large research firms are focusing on matured technologies, while our entire focus is on emerging technologies and markets. We at MnM, are helping companies to crack these complex changing business landscape, identify their new revenue sources and stay ahead of time and relevant.
