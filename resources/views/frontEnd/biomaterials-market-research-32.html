Biomaterials Market Research Reports & Consulting

Biomaterials are used to replace damaged tissues in the body; and are also used as surface coatings for medical implants and devices. Biomaterials are used extensively in wound-care applications due to their ability to initiate tissue regeneration. Many of these advanced materials are also biodegradable, that is, they dissolve in the body once their function is served.

The biomaterial market currently generates more than $30 billion globally, and is expected to grow at a double-digit CAGR in the next few years. Orthopedic applications form the largest segment of the overall biomaterials market. Polymer-based biomaterials are expected to initiate the next wave of market growth; and the emerging biochips and biosensors market segments also offer huge growth potential.

The display material market was valued at USD 28.12 Billion in 2016 and is expected to reach USD 34.60 Billion by 2023, at a CAGR of 3.1% during the forecast period. The market is driven by the large-scale adoption of LCD and OLED display panels.
